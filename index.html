About Me.

I am Sanjula Thilan, currently pursuing a BSc (Hons) in Software Engineering at NSBM Green University, Homagama. My academic journey has provided me with a solid foundation in both the theoretical and practical aspects of software development and UX engineering. Alongside my degree, I have enhanced my skill set through various educational programs, including a Web Designing program at the University of Moratuwa and a Diploma in Information Technology from ESOFT Metro Campus in Gampaha. Additionally, I have completed a Diploma in English and an AI/ML course at SLIIT University..

Beyond my academic pursuits, I have taken on leadership roles in student organizations, serving as the Vice President of both the Student Wellbeing Association and the NFORCE Society at NSBM Green University. These experiences have strengthened my leadership and teamwork abilities, while also reflecting my commitment to contributing positively to the community. My diverse educational background, coupled with my active participation in student organizations, showcases my well-rounded character and readiness to take on challenges in the field of UI/UX design.
